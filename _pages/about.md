About Me
======

Hello! My name is Andres Sanchez, and I am currently a PhD student and Research Assistant in the Civil and Environmental Engineering department at the University of Washington. My current research project is to develop probabalistic seismic risk models for the bridges and transportation networks in Washington State in the event of a Cascadia Subduction Zone (CSZ) rupture. This work is advised by [Prof. Jeffery Berman](https://www.ce.washington.edu/facultyfinder/jeffrey-w-berman) and [Prof. Marc Eberhard](https://www.ce.washington.edu/facultyfinder/marc-o-eberhard), and is part of the larger [Cascadia Coastlines and Peoples Hazards Research Hub](https://cascadiacopeshub.org/).
